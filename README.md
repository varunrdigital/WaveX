Project Title: WaveX Integrated Business Intelligence Suite
Key Highlights:
✅ Sales Intelligence: Built an executive view to track $23M+ in revenue, identifying top-performing products (WaveX R1/R2) and optimizing distributor performance.
✅ People Analytics: Designed an HR overview to monitor workforce health, analyzing everything from education levels to job satisfaction across departments.

Technical Deep-Dive:

ETL & Data Cleaning: Leveraged Power Query (M Language) to clean and shape messy datasets.

Data Modeling: Established a Star Schema, connecting Sales, Products, Categories, and HR data for seamless filtering.

Advanced DAX: Authored complex measures for Year-to-Date (YTD) sales, growth percentages, and satisfaction averages.

Interactive Design: Implemented dynamic filters and 10+ custom visualizations for a high-end UI/UX experience.
